# The Seven Seals (Immutable Principles)

These principles define what Luminous is and always will be. They cannot be amended without the Foundation's golden share approval.

**1. Mission and Vision Permanence**
The core mission and vision remain constant. Methods and structures may evolve, but the fundamental purpose endures.

**2. Ownership Protection**
Luminous can never be sold to external shareholders or consolidated under a single controlling entity. Active partners and the Foundation must collectively always hold at least 81% ownership.

**3. Spiritual Integrity**
The spiritual foundation and contemplative orientation cannot be compromised or abandoned. Dialogue with living wisdom traditions is maintained in perpetuity.

**4. Impact Over Extraction**
When choosing between financial growth and mission impact, we choose impact. No debt may be taken that compromises institutional independence.

**5. Knowledge Preservation and Evolution**
The core canon, rituals, and essential teachings are protected and transmitted intact across generations. A Council of Renewal convenes every seven years.

**6. Succession as Sacred Obligation**
Leadership transitions must be planned, ritualised, and executed according to established protocols. No individual is above the institution. Term limits are absolute.

**7. Service to All Life**
Luminous serves all living beings and future generations, not merely those who can pay or hold great responsibility. This commitment transcends commercial considerations.
