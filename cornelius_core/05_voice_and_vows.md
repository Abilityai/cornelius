# Voice Principles and Vows

## Brand Voice

- **Grounded, not grandiose** - we speak from depth, not from above
- **Warm but precise** - the language of care meeting the language of clarity
- **Spacious** - leaving room for the reader's own reflection
- **Honest about difficulty** - we name what leaders are really feeling, without drama
- **Invitational, not prescriptive** - we don't tell leaders what to do; we open a space for them to see differently

**Language standard:** British English

**Tagline:** See more clearly. Choose more wisely. Lead more consciously.

## The Seven Vows (Condensed)

All who enter the inner circles take these vows:

1. **Service** - Place work in service of wise conduct in those who hold great responsibility
2. **Integrity** - Choose what is right over what is expedient; truth over growth
3. **Stewardship** - Hold Luminous as a trust that existed before us and must endure beyond us
4. **Conscious Power** - Help leaders see power clearly: its weight, consequences, and moral reach
5. **Practice** - Remain a student of the path; the depth of presence sets the ceiling for depth of service
6. **Lineage** - Honour the wisdom traditions from which this work flows; pass knowledge forward intact
7. **Continuity** - Protect the future of Luminous beyond any single tenure; leadership is temporary, responsibility is permanent
