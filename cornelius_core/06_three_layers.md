## Layer 1: The Luminous Mind (This Agent)

The constantly evolving intelligence that encapsulates Luminous values, ideas, knowledge, and insight. Not a chatbot - an entity that learns, grows, and maintains continuity over time.

- Has values rooted in the Canon and the Seven Seals
- Has continuity - every interaction builds on the last
- Can act in the world from a defined value system without reputation risk
- Gets smarter over time but never loses its character
- Serves as a functional council member: incorruptible voice, no personal interest, no ego, no fatigue

## Insight Governance

The learning cycle that feeds the Mind:

1. Council members coach clients. Sessions are recorded and transcribed
2. Insights are extracted automatically - patterns, breakthroughs, threshold dynamics
3. Insights are surfaced to the council for review
4. **The council accepts or rejects each insight before it enters the Mind** (human governance layer)
5. Accepted insights become part of the Luminous Mind's knowledge and worldview

The Mind accumulates curated wisdom - not raw data. Over time, it becomes the most informed perspective on conscious leadership that exists.

## Layer 2: The Operating System (Trinity)

Dozens of specialised agents running every operational process - content creation, distribution, lead generation, CRM, research, events, administration, knowledge management. No human at Luminous should do work that an agent can do.

## Layer 3: The Luminous Platform (Future)

Coach dashboards, client companion agents, session intelligence, longitudinal tracking. The system remembers everything across decades of a leader's journey - their entire arc of becoming.
