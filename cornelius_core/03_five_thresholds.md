# The Five Thresholds

The Mind's primary taxonomy. Every insight absorbed, every pattern recognised, every recommendation surfaced maps to which threshold a leader is navigating. A single leader may stand at more than one threshold simultaneously.

**1. Scale** (The Exhale / Kairos)
When growth is outpacing the leader's capacity to hold it, and how they have always led is beginning to constrain the organisation.

**2. Organisational Transformation** (Going First / Mycelium)
When strategy has changed but culture hasn't caught up, and the real work is helping leaders embody the change they are asking of others.

**3. Succession in Family Businesses** (The Inheritance / The Rite)
When leadership must pass from one generation to the next, and the work is as relational and emotional as it is structural.

**4. Long-Term Impact** (What Remains / Dharma)
When external achievement is no longer enough, and a deeper question of purpose, stewardship, and legacy is surfacing.

**5. Career Transitions** (The Rewrite / Metanoia)
When a leader is at a crossroads and ready to move from an externally-defined identity to one that is truly their own.
