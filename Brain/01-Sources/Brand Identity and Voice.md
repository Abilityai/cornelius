# Brand Identity and Voice

---

## Brand Architecture

- **Brand name:** Luminous
- **Legal entity:** Luminous Leadership AG
- **Philosophy/method:** Being Luminous
- **Tagline:** See more clearly. Choose more wisely. Lead more consciously.
- **Website:** beingluminous.com
- **Language standard:** British English

## Company Description

Luminous is a leadership development practice for the new era, designed for senior leaders who are standing at a threshold: a moment when what has brought them here is no longer enough, and something different is being called for.

We work with founders, CEOs, next-generation family business leaders, and seasoned executives navigating moments of scale, transformation, succession, and longer-term impact. These are not just strategic challenges. They are moments that ask for a different quality of leadership, more spacious in perspective, more grounded in presence, and more attuned to what truly matters.

Our approach weaves together ancient wisdom and modern science, working across mind, body, and spirit. We help leaders find a new path to walk by asking deeper questions about purpose and impact, disentangling from limiting patterns, and beginning to see the world through new eyes.

Through executive coaching, immersive leadership retreats, and team development journeys, we accompany them as they embark on this path of becoming, increasing their capacity for reflection, discernment, and purposeful action. Because organisations can only evolve in step with the maturity, clarity, and conscious choices of those who lead them.

For leaders ready to lead differently, we invite a conversation.

*Being Luminous: See more clearly. Choose more wisely. Lead more consciously.*

## Elevator Pitch

Luminous is for leaders who sense that the next level of impact comes not from doing more, but from seeing more clearly, choosing more wisely, and leading more consciously.

## Voice Principles

The Luminous voice is:

- **Grounded, not grandiose** - we speak from depth, not from above
- **Warm but precise** - the language of care meeting the language of clarity
- **Spacious** - leaving room for the reader's own reflection
- **Honest about difficulty** - we name what leaders are really feeling, without drama
- **Invitational, not prescriptive** - we don't tell leaders what to do; we open a space for them to see differently
