# The Five Thresholds

*The moments that define Luminous's work with leaders*

---

Luminous serves leaders at five threshold moments - inflection points when what has brought them here no longer takes them where they need to go.

## 1. Scale

When growth is outpacing the leader's capacity to hold it, and the way they have always led is beginning to constrain the organisation.

**Working names:** The Exhale (brand identity) / Kairos (ancient wisdom)

## 2. Organisational Transformation

When strategy has changed but culture hasn't caught up, and the real work is helping leaders embody the change they are asking of others.

**Working names:** Going First (brand identity) / Mycelium (ancient wisdom)

## 3. Succession in Family Businesses

When leadership must pass from one generation to the next, and the work is as relational and emotional as it is structural.

**Working names:** The Inheritance (brand identity) / The Rite (ancient wisdom)

## 4. Long-Term Impact

When external achievement is no longer enough, and a deeper question of purpose, stewardship, and legacy is beginning to surface.

**Working names:** What Remains (brand identity) / Dharma (ancient wisdom)

## 5. Career Transitions

When a leader is at a crossroads - through choice or circumstance - and ready to move from an externally-defined identity to one that is truly their own.

**Working names:** The Rewrite (brand identity) / Metanoia (ancient wisdom)

---

## Note on the Framework

The thresholds are not diagnoses or categories. They are moments of consequence - when power concentrates, decisions reverberate, and the future bends. A single leader may stand at more than one threshold simultaneously.

The thresholds organize everything the Luminous Mind learns: every insight it absorbs, every pattern it recognizes, every recommendation it surfaces maps to which threshold a leader is navigating.
