# The Wave Model - how culture change actually spreads

## Core Framework

Culture change does not cascade - it waves. The cascade model pushes change top-down until it hits the bottom and stops. The wave model works differently: transformation spreads through warming, inspiring and involving, going out through the organisation and returning. Crucially, what comes back is carried forward in the next wave. Each cycle builds on what the previous one learned, making the process self-reinforcing rather than self-exhausting.

## The Distinction in Detail

**Cascade model**:
- Change is defined at the top and transmitted downward
- Energy depletes as it travels further from source
- Hits the bottom and stops - no return loop
- Generates workshops and fatigue within 12-18 months
- People disengage because they receive rather than participate

**Wave model**:
- Change spreads through warming, inspiring and involving
- Waves go out through the organisation and come back
- What returns is carried forward in the next wave
- Each cycle is richer than the last - a continuous, self-reinforcing loop
- Cultural ambassadors emerge organically; co-creation is built into the structure

---

## How Luminous Implements This

- Representative groups from across the organisation co-create the desired shift (not senior leadership defining and rolling out)
- Cultural workshops in different locations and departments create containers for honest dialogue
- Feedback loops return reflections and ideas to the centre
- Peer coaching and peer learning make self-examination habitual rather than occasional
- Cultural ambassadors spread the new way of being without top-down mandate
