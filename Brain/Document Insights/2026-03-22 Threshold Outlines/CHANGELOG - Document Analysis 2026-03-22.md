## Key Themes Identified

### Document 1: Organisational Transformations

1. **The Wave Model** - culture change is not a cascade but a self-reinforcing wave cycle; each cycle builds on the last
2. **CEO Readiness as Ceiling** - the leader's genuine willingness to be changed is the binding constraint on how far an organisation can go
3. **Regulation as Precondition** - nervous system regulation is Step 1, not a preparation; chronic stress creates the vicious cycle that blocks all downstream work
4. **Abdication vs. Empowerment** - a named and specific failure mode: leaders who declare empowerment without providing direction, safety, or genuine authority
5. **Depth as the Luminous Distinction** - the difference is not what Luminous does but how deep it is willing to go; surface behaviour vs. fears, assumptions, and patterns underneath

### Document 2: Succession

1. **Founder as the Harder Conversation** - the founding generation is typically the real locus of blockage; founder willingness shapes everything
2. **Obligation vs. Choice** - the heir who never genuinely chose; first work is discerning obligation from authentic desire
3. **Transference of Founder Fear** - irrational poverty fear absorbed by wealthy heirs from founders who built from scarcity
4. **Next-Gen Motivational Shift** - consistent generational pattern away from survival/status toward impact, stewardship, spiritual meaning
5. **Succession Is Systemic** - individual coaching has structural limits without family system work
6. **The Governance Gap** - multi-heir structures cannot use the standard "elect one CEO" solution

### Document 3: Renewal and Transition

1. **Quiet Completion** - distinct from burnout; a sense of nothing left to prove, no map for what's next
2. **Provider Identity Collapse** - for many men, the deepest identity layer; crumbles when threatened
3. **Unprocessed Grief** - anger, loss, sadness not yet named; must be worked with before planning begins
4. **Financial Maths Nobody Does** - the actual number needed is almost always lower than assumed; even finance professionals have not done this calculation for themselves
5. **Renewal Without Leaving** - some clients discover they can return to their role differently
6. **Try It On** - experiential discernment through safe test environments (volunteering, board roles, a month at a vineyard)
7. **Family as Projected Blocker** - fear of letting down loved ones rarely matched by reality; the family is more supportive than expected
8. **The 18-Month Arc** - patience and breaks are part of the methodology, not deviations from it
9. **Community Mapping for Discovery** - network activated for learning, not job-hunting; changes the nature of every conversation
