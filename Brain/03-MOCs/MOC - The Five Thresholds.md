# MOC - The Five Thresholds

The Five Thresholds are the organising framework for all of Luminous's work. They map the terrain of inner and outer transformation that leaders navigate at critical junctures - and define the domains where Luminous can intervene most effectively.

---

## The Thresholds

Each threshold names a domain of transformation, pairing a working title with its deeper lineage in ancient wisdom.

1. [[The First Threshold - Identity]] - Who you are beneath the role: the Homeric confrontation with the self behind the mask
2. [[The Second Threshold - Power]] - How you hold and exercise influence: the Stoic question of what is truly yours to command
3. [[The Third Threshold - Relationships]] - The quality of your connections: the Confucian domain of right relationship and mutual cultivation
4. [[The Fourth Threshold - Purpose]] - What you are building and why: the Vedantic inquiry into dharma and the life that is authentically yours
5. [[The Fifth Threshold - Legacy]] - What you leave behind: the Egyptian concept of the weighing of the heart - what endures when you are gone

---

## The Taxonomy

Why the thresholds are structured as they are.

- [[The thresholds are the Mind's taxonomy]] - The Five Thresholds are not arbitrary categories but a map of how consciousness organises its deepest questions; they structure both the client journey and the intelligence layer
