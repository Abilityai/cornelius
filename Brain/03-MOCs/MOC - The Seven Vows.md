# MOC - The Seven Vows

The Seven Vows are the sacred commitments undertaken by Luminous practitioners. They are not a code of conduct imposed from outside but a voluntary alignment with the values that make the work possible. Each vow names a dimension of the practitioner identity.

---

## The Vows

The seven commitments, in order.

1. [[The Vow of Service]] - To place the client's becoming above the practitioner's recognition or reward
2. [[The Vow of Integrity]] - To act with wholeness: no gap between private conduct and public practice
3. [[The Vow of Stewardship]] - To hold the institution, its knowledge, and its resources as a trust for future generations
4. [[The Vow of Conscious Power]] - To wield influence with full awareness of its effects and full accountability for its use
5. [[The Vow of Practice]] - To remain a student: to practise what is taught and teach only what is practised
6. [[The Vow of Lineage]] - To honour those who came before and invest in those who come after
7. [[The Vow of Continuity]] - To act in ways that strengthen the institution across time, not just within one's own tenure

---

## Practitioner Identity

The vows are expressions of a deeper identity.

- [[Craftsmen of consciousness - always in training never complete]] - The vows are not achieved once; they are renewed through daily practice and honest self-assessment
