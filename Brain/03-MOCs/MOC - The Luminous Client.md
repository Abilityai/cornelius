# MOC - The Luminous Client

Luminous does not serve everyone. It serves a specific kind of person at a specific kind of moment - a leader standing at a threshold, sensing that the next chapter requires a different quality of self. This MOC maps what we know about who they are, what they carry, and how they arrive.

---

## The Archetype

The defining portrait of the person Luminous is built for.

- [[The leader at a threshold - archetype]] - The full archetype: accomplished, credible, and quietly aware that the skills that built their success may not be the ones that will sustain it

## The Experience

What it feels like to be this person before they find Luminous.

- [[What brought them here no longer works and they sense it]] - The internal signal that precedes the search: not crisis but a growing dissonance between outer success and inner coherence
- [[The brave face that rarely comes down]] - The performance of certainty that high-achieving leaders maintain even as private doubt accumulates
- [[Searching outside for meaning and never finding it]] - The loop of achievement, recognition, and disappointment that eventually drives the turn inward
